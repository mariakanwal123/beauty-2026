About Maria Kanwal

Beauty Expert & Wellness Advocate

Welcome to my beauty sanctuary. I'm Maria Kanwal — a certified beauty therapist, skincare formulator, and wellness coach with over a decade of hands-on experience in the beauty industry across Asia and Europe.

My philosophy is rooted in evidence-based skincare and sustainable beauty practices. I believe that authentic radiance is cultivated through consistent, mindful care — not expensive products or complicated routines. Every article I write is tested, researched, and written with your real lifestyle in mind.

Through this platform I've helped thousands of women simplify their routines, understand their skin, and build lasting confidence. Whether you're a skincare beginner or a beauty enthusiast, there's something here for you.
